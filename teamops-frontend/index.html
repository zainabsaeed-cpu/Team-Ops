Core features

Built for teams who
ship fast

Real-Time Sync
Socket.io broadcasts every card move and comment to all connected clients in under 50ms.
Kanban Boards
Drag-and-drop cards across custom workflow stages. Position persisted in PostgreSQL.
Role-Based Access
Owner, Admin, Member roles. JWT auth with bcrypt password hashing throughout.
Live Notifications
Get pinged on assignments and @mentions. Badge counts and a full notification history page.
Activity Feed
Every action logged and surfaced live in a sidebar — who moved what, when, with context.

Relational storage with JOINs, FK constraints, ordered positions, and indexed queries.

Resources

FAQ

Quick answers for common TeamOps workflows.

Getting Started

Open the workspace menu, choose Create Workspace, add a name, then save it.
Go to Members, copy the invite code, and share it with the people you want to add.
Use Join Workspace from the workspace menu and paste the invite code you received.

Boards & Cards

Open the board selector and choose New Board, then enter the board name.
Click Add Card in the column where the work should start, fill in the details, and save.
Drag the card to another column or open the card and change its status.
Open the card, use the assignee field, and select a workspace member.
Open the card details and update the due date and priority fields.
Open the board comments panel, type your comment, and send it.
Use the card tag option in the comment composer, then choose the card you want to reference.

Members & Roles

Owners control the workspace, admins manage team settings, members collaborate on boards, and viewers have read-only access.
Go to Members, find the teammate, and update their role from the role selector.
A Board Leader is the main person responsible for keeping a board organized and moving.
Specialty tags describe a member's focus area so teams can quickly find the right person for work.

Notifications & Activity

TeamOps sends notifications for important workspace and board updates, including assigned work and collaboration events.
Open Activity from the sidebar to see the latest workspace timeline.

Settings

Open Profile, enter your current password and new password, then save the change.
Open Profile, choose a new image, then save your profile.
Workspace deletion is handled from Settings by users with owner-level access.
Still need help? Contact us at support@teamops.dev
